Frequently Asked Questions

How many styles can I choose from?

We currently have an option of 3, however please contact us for any custom orders. We create each quilt by hand and are happy to accomodate requests.

How do I pay?

We will send you the final quote and a payment link after we process the order. All our quilts are $200 AUD with free shipping within Australia.

How long does it take?

It depends on our workload, however we aim to complete each order within a week. Please allow additional time for shipping.

Are the quilts machine washable?

Yes! All our quilts can be washed on a 40 degree cold cycle with mild detergent. When selecting materials we always ensure that the colour doesn't run.
